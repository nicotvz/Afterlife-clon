AFTERLIFE is the record label of the duo Tale of Us (consisting of Matteo Milleri and Carmine Conte). At the same time, Afterlife, is presented as a massive event, where the DJs that belong to the firm are staged live. These shows offer an incredible staging around the world, since they have effects of lights and screens of last generation, where the futuristic visual aspects offer the audience a unique experience.

The symbol of Afterlife represents man throwing himself towards infinity.

"The concept comes from our need to explore different realities. The man who falls represents our journey into the unknown. A search, an exploration, a call to innovation. It is also a parallel with the meaning of life, we trust that there is a meaning." -Matteo
